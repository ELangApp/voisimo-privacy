Privacy Policy for Voisimo

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Introduction

Voisimo ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use Voisimo, we collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Display Name: Your chosen name shown to your contacts
  • User ID: Automatically generated identifier for your account

Contact Information

  • Contact List: Phone numbers of people you add as contacts (stored locally on your device and on our servers)

Call Data

  • Call Metadata: Call duration, participants, timestamps
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version for technical support

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Phone numbers (registration is anonymous)
  • ❌ Call audio or video recordings
  • ❌ Message contents (we don't have messaging)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Profile pictures or photos

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Enable voice and video calling between users
  • User Authentication: Verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access
  • Push Notifications: Alert you about incoming calls
  • Technical Support: Troubleshoot issues and improve app performance
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Data

  • Server Location: Our servers are securely hosted in the cloud
  • Local Storage: Contact lists are also stored locally on your device

How We Protect Data

  • ✅ HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • ✅ Encrypted storage of passwords and sensitive data
  • ✅ Real-time call data is end-to-end encrypted via Agora SDK
  • ✅ No recording of call audio or video
  • ✅ Regular security audits and updates

Third-Party Services

FamilyCall uses the following third-party services:

Agora.io (Real-Time Communication)

  • Purpose: Provides real-time video and audio calling infrastructure
  • Data Shared: User ID, channel information
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.agora.io/en/privacy-policy/

Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)

  • Purpose: Delivers push notifications for incoming calls
  • Data Shared: Device token, user ID
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Call Metadata: Retained for 90 days for service improvement
  • Call Content: Not recorded or stored
  • After Account Deletion: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

Children's Privacy

Voisimo is designed for family use, including children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Voisimo is available globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this privacy policy, regardless of your location.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending a notification for significant changes

Your continued use of Voisimo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Analytics and Cookies

Voisimo does not use cookies or third-party analytics services. We only collect the data necessary to provide our calling services.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email or in-app notification.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: voisimoapp@gmail.com
  • Website: https://voisimo.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 7 business days.
